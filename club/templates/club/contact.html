Overview

Club Network is a web application that aims to make it easy for people who are new to an area or city to find and join activity clubs and provides a system for activity club managers to advertise their clubs.

Advantages

Club Network provides a solution to a problem many individuals face when moving to a new area or city where making connections can be a challenge, also making it simple to attract more members.

About us

We are students of Group 49 of the course COMPSCI5012 Internet Technology at Glasgow University and this is our first web application. We put a lot of effort into collaborating on this, hope you will like it!
